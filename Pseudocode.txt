Summary

1. The game is called rock paper scissor.
2. The computer will play the game by choosing between the three words (i.e rock, paper or scissor) after user input.
3. The user will input a Rock, paper or scissor in javascript console.
4. Rock beats scissor, scissor beat paper, paper beat rock.
5. The computer will generate the words randomly.
6. The result will be evaluated based on the player choice and computer choice.
7. If one of them win once in a round that round is won.
8. There are 5 rounds in which both user and computer can play.
9. There are 5 rounds in one game.
9. The player with highest amount of wins per game will ultimately win.
